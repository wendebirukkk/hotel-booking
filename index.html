About Green Pearl

Your Home in Addis Ababa

Green Pearl Hotel is a cozy, highly-rated 3-star accommodation located in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. Situated in Gulele Sub City, Woreda 7, right next to Hamle 19 School in Addisu Gebeya, it is well-known for its comfortable rooms, welcoming terrace, a café, and excellent local dining options including traditional fasting buffets.

The hotel is approximately 7 miles from Bole International Airport, making it a convenient and comfortable base for both business and leisure travellers visiting Addis Ababa.

GREEN PEARL HOTEL provides the best in services and amenities to help make guests feel comfortable. Guestrooms are designed to provide an optimal level of comfort with welcoming decor, free Wi-Fi, daily housekeeping, linen service, blackout curtains, and air conditioning in select rooms.
